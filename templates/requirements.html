ELIGIBILITY

Carefully, read the following to know the burasry application REQUIREMENTS.

You MUST be a native residence of Kilifi in order to qualify for the awarding of the bursary.
Fill out the application form completely and accurately. Provide all required information. Be honest and transparent in your responses.
For new students, ensure you attach a copy of your Primary/Secondary school result slip.
Have a good academic performance at the secondary or tertiary level.
Submit authentic birth certificate for secondary school and National ID for tertiary level applicants.
All inaccurately filled forms will be automatically disqualify the application.
Newly admitted students should include a copy of the Admission letter.
NOTE: Review your application for errors, typos, and grammatical mistakes. A well-written and error-free application reflects positively on your candidacy.
